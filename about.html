BanglaCHI

BanglaCHI is the community of Bangladeshi HCI enthusiasts all over the world. HCI enthusiasts of Bangladesh constantly publish marvelous research papers in renowned human factors venues as CHI, CSCW, UbiComp, and others. The researchers of BanglaCHI worked/currently work with technological giants such as Google, Microsoft, Facebook, IBM, etc. In 2017, a few motivated minds took the initiative to work together beneath an umbrella, and though strong vision, there formed Dhaka ACM SIGCHI Chapter first time in Bangladesh. Now, this community is expanding, organizing, and attending multiple international HCI events actively from 2019 to present.

Vision: If there is an HCI researcher, he/she will able to make ten more HCI researchers. BanglaCHI is dreaming to come up with one of the strongest HCI communities in the world.
